Another Week, Another Project

Time to dive into another project. Monday always seems to be an exciting day here at the guild. There is this buzzing kind of energy. People rushing around to review prior week projects, then obtaining new projects and a new team mate for pair programming. It almost makes Monday not so bad (however I still dislike waking up early on Monday haha). Let’s see what code awaits this week.

Advanced JavaScript

This week I will be working on the project goal titled Advanced JavaScript. There are a variety of topics covered within the project goal, such as Node.JS tutorials, Express, object-oriented programming, and the module pattern. There are also suggested tutorials for additional review tutorials related to loops/arrays/objects, JQuery, and the DOM.

Time constraints

After reviewing the project specs, it is definitely a lot of information to cover. My team mate has suggested to cover 3 topics per day, and to approach the project as a general overview/introduction. This sounds like a good game plan to me, and I am looking forward to working on this project.
